## Improvements Made

### New Obstacle Types
1. **Gravity Well** (`gravitywell.ts`)
   - Circular attractor that pulls ball toward center
   - Animated concentric rings (pulsing inward), floating core orb, orbiting torus
   - Force scales inversely with distance from center
   - Point light + eerie warping SFX
   - Added to Course 2 Hole 9, Course 3 Holes 5 and 9

2. **Speed Boost Pad** (`gravitywell.ts`)
   - Directional pad that accelerates ball on contact
   - Animated chevrons flowing in boost direction
   - Edge wireframe + point light
   - Cooldown prevents repeated triggers
   - Added to Course 2 Hole 9, Course 3 Holes 7 and 9

### Visual Polish (postfx.ts)
3. **Scanline/CRT Overlay**
   - Subtle horizontal scanlines (every 4px) + faint vertical grid
   - Radial vignette darkening edges
   - `mix-blend-mode: overlay` at 6% opacity for minimal intrusiveness
   - Reinforces holodeck aesthetic without hurting readability

4. **Camera Shake System**
   - Triggered on: wall bounces, bumper hits, OOB, water hazard, hole-in-one
   - Intensity proportional to impact force
   - Exponential decay for natural feel
   - Applied as position offset to orbit camera

5. **Slow-Motion Effect**
   - Triggers when ball is <0.3m from hole and moving slowly (dramatic near-miss moments)
   - Time scale smoothly lerps to 0.4x, then returns to 1.0x
   - Applied to all game systems (physics, effects, obstacles) but NOT input/UI

6. **Ball Spin Animation**
   - Mesh rotation proportional to velocity direction and speed
   - X-axis rotation from Z-velocity, Z-axis rotation from X-velocity
   - Makes the ball feel physically grounded

7. **Enhanced Ball Glow**
   - Glow intensity increases with ball speed
   - Light color shifts to brighter cyan at high speeds
   - Creates satisfying visual feedback loop for powerful shots

### Audio Improvements
8. **Rolling Ball Sound**
   - Brownian noise through bandpass filter
   - Volume and center frequency scale with ball speed
   - Smooth transitions (no pops/clicks)

9. **Distinct Score SFX**
   - Eagle: dramatic ascending 6-note arpeggio + double sparkle
   - Birdie: bright chirpy ascending pattern
   - Par: clean satisfying two-tone confirmation
   - Bogey: slightly disappointed descending tone

10. **New Obstacle SFX**
    - Gravity well: eerie descending warping tone
    - Speed boost: ascending filtered noise whoosh + tone cascade
    - Streak chime: escalating tones based on streak length

### Game Feel
11. **Streak/Combo System**
    - Tracks consecutive under-par holes within a course
    - Shows toast notification at 3+ streak ("HOT STREAK! ×3")
    - Escalates: "ON FIRE! ×5", "UNSTOPPABLE! ×7", "PERFECT ROUND!"
    - Accompanied by escalating chime SFX
    - Resets on course start or over-par hole

## Technical Notes
- All new features integrate cleanly with existing XR controller system
- Gravity well force is additive with wind zones
- Speed boost goes through same zone-checking pipeline as teleporters/wind/ice
- Camera shake offset applied after camera position lerp, before lookAt
- Slow motion scales game dt but NOT input dt (preserves controller responsiveness)
- Build requires Node 20+ (nvm use 20) due to Vite 7.3 crypto.hash requirement
